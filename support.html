Support & FAQs

Everything you need to know about Alo Translate.

🚀

Getting Started

How does Alo Translate work?▼
Alo Translate is an AI-powered real-time voice interpreter. Choose your source and target languages, tap the microphone button, and speak naturally. The app transcribes your speech, translates it using AI, then plays the translated audio aloud.
Which languages are supported?▼
Alo Translate supports 40+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, and many more.
Do I need an account or internet connection?▼
No account or login is required. An internet connection is needed only for live translation and voice-to-text features. Your profiles and settings are stored locally and work offline.
🔒

Privacy & Data

Is my personal information safe?▼
Yes. All personal profile information and conversation history are stored securely and exclusively on your physical device using iOS Keychain Services. We do not operate external user databases or cloud sync for personal data.
How can I delete my data?▼
Open the Profile Sheet (person icon top right), go to the "C\u00e0i \u0111\u1eb7t chung" (General Settings) tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap "X\u00f3a t\u00e0i kho\u1ea3n & D\u1eef li\u1ec7u" (Delete Account & Data) under Account & Privacy. This permanently clears all data from your device and cannot be undone.
What data is sent to the internet?▼
Only the audio/text needed for real-time translation is sent, and only at the moment of translation. These requests are stateless — no data is retained by the provider after the translation is returned.
🛠️

Troubleshooting

The translation isn't working.▼
1) Ensure you have an active internet connection. 2) Check microphone and speech recognition permissions in Settings → Privacy. 3) Try speaking more clearly. 4) Restart the app. If the issue persists, email our support team.
The microphone button is not responding.▼
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and ensure Alo Translate is enabled. Also check Speech Recognition permissions. If already granted, try force-closing and reopening the app.
The keyboard covers the text fields in my profile.▼
The profile sheet is fully scrollable. When the keyboard appears, scroll up within the sheet to see and interact with the field you're editing. The sheet resizes automatically to keep your content accessible.

Still need help?

Our team is responsive and happy to assist with any issue or question you have.
