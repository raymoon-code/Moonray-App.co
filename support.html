Support & FAQs

Everything you need to know about Alo Translate.

🚀

Getting Started

How does Alo Translate work? ▼
Alo Translate is an AI-powered real-time voice interpreter. Choose your source and target languages, tap the microphone button, and speak naturally. The app transcribes your speech, translates it using AI, then plays the translated audio aloud — enabling seamless two-way conversations.
Which languages are supported? ▼
Alo Translate supports 40+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese, Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, and many more. The full list is available in the language selector within the app.
Do I need an account or internet connection? ▼
No account or login is required. An internet connection is needed only for the live translation and voice-to-text features, which use secure, stateless API requests. Your profiles and settings are stored locally on your device and work offline.
🔒

Privacy & Data

Is my personal information safe? ▼
Yes, absolutely. All personal profile information (Name, Date of Birth, Gender, etc.) and conversation history are stored securely and exclusively on your physical device using iOS Keychain Services. We do not operate external user databases or cloud sync for personal data.
How can I delete my data? ▼
Open the Profile Sheet (person icon at the top right of the main screen), navigate to the "Cài đặt chung" (General Settings) tab, and scroll to the bottom. Under "Account & Privacy", tap "Xóa tài khoản & Dữ liệu" (Delete Account & Data). This permanently clears all profiles, settings, and conversation history from your device. This action cannot be undone.
What data is sent to the internet? ▼
Only the audio/text needed for real-time translation is sent over the internet, and only at the moment of translation. These requests are stateless — the API does not associate your voice or text with your identity, and no data is retained by the provider after the translation is returned.
🛠️

Troubleshooting

The translation isn't working. What should I do? ▼
1) Ensure you have an active internet connection. 2) Check that microphone and speech recognition permissions are granted in Settings → Privacy → Microphone/Speech Recognition. 3) Try speaking more clearly and slightly slower. 4) Restart the app. If the issue persists, email our support team.
The microphone button is not responding. ▼
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and ensure Alo Translate is enabled. Also check Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition. If permissions are already granted, try force-closing the app and reopening it.
The keyboard is covering the text fields when editing my profile. ▼
The profile sheet is fully scrollable. When the keyboard appears, scroll up within the sheet to see and interact with the field you're editing. The sheet will resize automatically to keep your content accessible.
