Alo Translate - Support & FAQs

If you are using Alo Translate, here are the answers to frequently asked questions and how to request support.

1. How does Alo Translate work?

Alo Translate is an AI-powered voice interpreter. Simply choose your target language, tap a microphone button, and speak. The app transcribes, translates, and plays the audio translation aloud to facilitate seamless two-way conversations.

2. Is my personal information safe?

Yes, absolutely. All personal profile information, such as your optional personal profile fields (Name, Date of Birth, Gender, etc.) and your conversation history, is stored securely and locally on your physical device. We do not store your data on external databases or servers.

3. Do I need an account to use the app?

No account or login is required to use any translation features. The app is fully functional offline/locally for stored configurations, and uses transient, stateless APIs solely for voice-to-text and translation processing.

4. How can I delete my data?

You can easily wipe all of your data at any time. Open the profile sheet (person icon at the top right), navigate to the "Cài đặt chung" (General Settings) tab, and scroll to the bottom. Under "Account & Privacy", tap "Xóa tài khoản & Dữ liệu" (Delete Account & Data). This will completely and permanently clear all profiles and conversations from your device.

5. What should I do if the translation fails?

Ensure you have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try speaking more clearly or checking that your microphone permissions are enabled in your device settings.
