Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

1. Overview

Alo Translate ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. Alo Translate is built to operate as a local-first voice interpretation utility. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information you input into the App.

2. Data Storage Policy (On-Device Only)

Your privacy is our core priority. All personal profiles, custom fields, and conversation histories are stored exclusively on your physical device using secure, encrypted local storage (via Keychain Services on iOS). We do not run external user databases or sync your personal details to cloud profiles.

3. Optional Personal Information Collection

To assist with translations during medical, tax, legal, or rental consultations, the App allows you to pre-fill common personal fields (such as Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Phone Number, Home Address, and Emergency Contact). All of these fields are entirely optional. You are not required to provide any personal details to use the primary translation and voice interpretation features of the App. If provided, this data is only used as conversational context when translating to help the AI helper suggest accurate responses.

4. Transient Processing for Translation and AI

To provide translation and AI follow-up features, voice recordings are converted to text and text inputs are translated using secure, transient APIs. These requests are stateless; they do not associate inputs with your personal identity, and the service providers do not retain your input text or voice data for model training or permanent storage.

5. Device Permissions

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Microphone: Required to capture speech for real-time transcription and translation.
  • Speech Recognition: Required to translate spoken words into text.

You may revoke these permissions at any time via your device's settings.

6. Complete Account and Data Deletion

We provide you with full control over your data. Because all data is stored on-device, you can permanently erase it at any time directly within the App:

  • Open the Profile Sheet (person icon at the top right).
  • Select the Cài đặt chung (General Settings) tab.
  • Scroll to the bottom and click Xóa tài khoản & Dữ liệu (Delete Account & Data) under the "Account & Privacy" section.
Confirming this action will immediately and permanently erase all secure storage keys, personalized profiles, settings, and conversation histories from your physical device. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
