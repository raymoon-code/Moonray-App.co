Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not an afterthought — it's our foundation.

✅ Effective Date: June 15, 2026

1. Overview

Short version: Alo Translate stores everything locally on your device. We don't run user databases, we don't sell data, and we don't track you.

Alo Translate ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information you provide in the App and what data, if any, leaves your device.

2. On-Device Data Storage

All personal profiles, custom fields, and conversation histories are stored exclusively on your physical device using secure, encrypted local storage (iOS Keychain Services). We do not operate external user databases, cloud sync, or any remote profile storage for personal information.

3. Optional Personal Information

To assist with accurate contextual translations, the App allows you to optionally pre-fill common personal fields such as Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Phone Number, Home Address, and Emergency Contact.

All fields are entirely optional. If provided, data is only used locally as conversational context. It is never sent to external servers or stored outside your device.

4. Transient Processing for Translation

To provide real-time translation, voice recordings are converted to text and text is translated via secure, encrypted API calls. These requests are stateless — they do not associate your input with your personal identity, and service providers do not retain your data for model training or permanent storage.

5. Device Permissions

  • Microphone: Required to capture speech for real-time transcription and translation.
  • Speech Recognition: Required to convert spoken words into text for translation.

You may revoke these permissions at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security on your device.

6. Complete Data Deletion

To permanently erase all data stored by the App:

1
Open the Profile Sheet (person icon at top-right of the main screen).
2
Navigate to the C\u00e0i \u0111\u1eb7t chung (General Settings) tab.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap X\u00f3a t\u00e0i kho\u1ea3n & D\u1eef li\u1ec7u (Delete Account & Data) under "Account & Privacy".

This immediately and permanently erases all profiles, settings, and conversation histories from your device. This action cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

Alo Translate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information via the App, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
