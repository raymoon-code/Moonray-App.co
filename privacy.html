Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not an afterthought — it's our foundation.

✅ Effective Date: June 15, 2026

1. Overview

Short version: Alo Translate stores everything locally on your device. We don't run user databases, we don't sell data, and we don't track you.

Alo Translate ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information you provide in the App and what data, if any, leaves your device.

2. On-Device Data Storage

All personal profiles, custom fields, and conversation histories are stored exclusively on your physical device using secure, encrypted local storage (iOS Keychain Services). We do not operate external user databases, cloud sync, or any remote profile storage for personal information.

This means your data is under your full control at all times — only you and your device have access to it.

3. Optional Personal Information

To assist with accurate contextual translations in medical, legal, or administrative settings, the App allows you to optionally pre-fill common personal fields such as:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Emergency Contact

All fields are entirely optional. You are not required to provide any personal details to use the translation or voice interpretation features. If provided, data is only used locally as conversational context to help the AI generate accurate, relevant translations.

4. Transient Processing for Translation

To provide real-time translation, voice recordings are converted to text and text is translated via secure, encrypted API calls. These requests are stateless — they do not associate your voice or text input with your personal identity, and the service providers do not retain your input data for model training or permanent storage.

Audio and text sent for translation is processed in-transit only and is not stored on any server.

5. Device Permissions

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Microphone: Required to capture speech for real-time transcription and translation.
  • Speech Recognition: Required to convert spoken words into text for translation.

You may revoke these permissions at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security on your device. Revoking permissions will disable voice features but will not affect locally stored profiles or settings.

6. Complete Data Deletion

You have full control over all data stored by the App. To permanently erase everything, follow these steps:

1
Open the Profile Sheet by tapping the person icon at the top-right of the main screen.
2
Navigate to the Cài đặt chung (General Settings) tab.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap Xóa tài khoản & Dữ liệu (Delete Account & Data) under "Account & Privacy".

Confirming this action immediately and permanently erases all secure storage keys, personalized profiles, settings, and conversation histories from your device. This action cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

Alo Translate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information via the App, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the App or via the App Store update notes. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
